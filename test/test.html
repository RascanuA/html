My little bakery

From hobby to bussine

It all started with my mother's pleasure in making home made cookies and cakes for me and my brother,since she knew they are a little bit more healthy then the store both ones. There were friends and family member that came to our birthday parties and enjoy the sweet she was making and started asking her to make some for them. From there it all evolved from person to person she started making caks and sweets for more and more people.

Since more and more people started to discover her sweet goods we developed the idea of making a small bussines out of it.I joined her since i was very patient/manual she made me discover the sugar paste that helps you create alot of different decorations for all sorts of sweets and cakes from there we started making more and more complex themes for different events.
